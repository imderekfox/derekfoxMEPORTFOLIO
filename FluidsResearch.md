## The Research

When a less viscous fluid (water + CaCl₂) displaces a more viscous one 
(1.1 wt% sodium alginate) in a Hele-Shaw cell, the interface destabilizes into 
branching finger patterns. The lab investigated how **gel formation at the 
fluid interface** — driven by a sodium alginate + CaCl₂ cross-linking reaction — 
could be used to actively control and manipulate those patterns.
